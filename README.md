# Bet On It Scene
This is a game adaptation of the Bet On It musical scene from High School Musical 2. The player has to use certain items/tasks to progress through the song.
# Reference

# Walkthrough
1. The player interacts with the flyer, then walks to the golf field.
2. Player interacts with NPCs and obtains grass to continue to the rocks.
3. Player interacts with the rocks and obtains a rock to jump off the rocks and go to the bottom.
4. Player interacts and obtains sand to progress to the golf course.
5. Player interacts with more NPCs and obtains a golf club to go to the water.
6. Player interacts with water and obtains water droplets to progress to the top of the golf field.
7. Player gets to the top of the golf field and wins.
